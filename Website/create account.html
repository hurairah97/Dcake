About us

The Cake is a European concept bakery founded in 2014 to bring the authentic taste of European home baking to Karachi. Our cakes, cupcakes and brownies owe their unique look and exceptional flavor to top quality raw ingredients, our baking expertise, and a passionate team of people who love to live their lives by the oven.
